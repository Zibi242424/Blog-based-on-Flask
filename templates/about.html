About

Let me introduce myself.

My name is Zbyszek and I study electronics at Wroclaw University of Science and Technology in Poland. This website is a project which helped me to learn web developing however I will regularly post new things concerning my hobbys such as music, mixology or technology. For the most part I will post some music reviews both in Polish and English. I love sharing new music with other people so I hope that You will find some new artists thanks to this website. I truly believe that You wil find this website useful and entertaining. If You find something that can be improved or changed, please contact me.

All the website's background logic is written in Python. As a framework I used Flask with SQLAlchemy in order to manage the database. For sure You can notice that there's a lot of Bootstrap, mainly Jumbotron. I'm not a big fan of coding in HTML and CSS so I wanted to make the look of the website simple and clear as possible. Nevertheless I hope my blog isn't to ugly and You will enjoy visit here. :)
